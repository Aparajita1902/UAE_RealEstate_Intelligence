This month's brief

Cash is the headline. AED 27.8M sits overdue across 135 buyers, but a tenth of the apparent delinquency is not delinquency: nine buyers have AED 1.55M of collected cash sitting unapplied against their invoices. Reconciling those receipts is the fastest recovery available this month, ahead of the 20 collection calls queued below. Nine buyers have crossed three missed instalments and now sit on termination watch.

On revenue, the discount ladder is a habit rather than a lever: deals above 5% sold no faster than deals at 2 to 5%, and that habit has cost AED 10.7M since launch. Road-facing large units are stagnating, with T2 2BRs at 0.11 sales per month; a 4 to 6% reprice now beats a distress cut at handover. Meanwhile 27 token-only reservations are holding AED 54.9M of stock beyond policy, and 16 live bookings carry the cancellation signature (10%+ discount on token) that historically cancels at 50%. Registration needs one batch push: 47 executed SPAs remain outside Oqood past the 60-day line.
